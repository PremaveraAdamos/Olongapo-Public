Olongapo, officially the City of Olongapo (Sambali: Syodad in Olongapo Filipino: Lungsod ng Olongapo) and often referred to as Olongapo City, is a highly urbanized city located in the province of Zambales, Philippines. It has a population of 221,178 people according to the 2010 census. Along with the town of Subic, it comprises Metro Olongapo, one of the twelve metropolitan areas in the Philippines
